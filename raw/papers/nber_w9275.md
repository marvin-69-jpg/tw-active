# Mutual Fund Flows and Performance in Rational Markets — Berk-Green (2002 NBER WP / 2004 JPE)

## Abstract（原文）

We develop a simple rational model of active portfolio management that
provides a natural benchmark against which to evaluate observed relationship
between returns and fund flows. We show that many effects widely regarded as
anomalous are consistent with this simple explanation. In the model,
investments with active managers do not outperform passive benchmarks because
of the competitive market for capital provision, combined with decreasing
returns to scale in active portfolio management. Consequently, past
performance cannot be used to predict future returns, or to infer the average
skill level of active managers. The lack of persistence in active manager
returns does not imply that differential ability across managers is
nonexistent or unrewarded, that gathering information about performance is
socially wasteful, or that chasing performance is pointless. A strong
relationship between past performance and the flow of funds exists in our
model—indeed this is the market mechanism that ensures that no predictability
in performance exists. Calibrating the model to the fund flows and
survivorship rates, we find these features of the data are consistent with
the vast majority (80%) of active managers having at least enough skill to
make back their fees.

## 重點 takeaways（精讀後筆記）

1. **這是 PSTZ 系列的「理論母題」**——decreasing returns to scale 是模型的**外生假設**，不是實證；PSTZ 2014 才在資料裡實證**哪個層級**（fund vs industry）有 diseconomies。

2. **核心 mechanism：競爭性資金供給 + 規模遞減 → 平均 alpha = 0**
   - 投資人 rational + 完全競爭 → 任何被觀察到的 skill 都會吸走資金
   - 直到該基金規模大到 alpha 被規模成本吃光、預期超額報酬 = 0
   - **均衡時 alpha 不持續、無法預測**——但**不是因為經理人沒技能**，是因為市場把技能 rent 全部 capture 給經理人（透過 management fee）

3. **flow → performance 的負相關是 feature 不是 bug**
   - 「資金追逐表現」傳統上被當作非理性 anomaly
   - Berk-Green：這就是 rational equilibrium 的 enforcement mechanism
   - 觀察到 flow 強烈反應 lagged return = 市場有效率的證據，**不是**反證

4. **Calibration 結論：80% 主動經理人有 skill 到能 cover fee**
   - 這個數字常被誤解為「主動投資不值得」——其實 Berk-Green 在說 active management is **socially valuable**，只是 rent 都被經理人收走，投資人事後拿不到 alpha

5. **對台灣主動 ETF 的 implication**
   - 若 Berk-Green 在台灣 hold，主動 ETF 規模一上來，**不論經理人有多 skilled，平均淨報酬都會收斂到 benchmark**
   - 投資人想跑贏，理論上唯一勝算是「比別的投資人更早識別 skilled manager」——這在 daily basket 揭露的主動 ETF 結構下有沒有可行性，是一個 open question

## 跟 PSTZ 2014 的關係

- Berk-Green 2004 是**理論**（rational benchmark model）
- PSTZ 2014 是**實證**（fund-level vs industry-level diseconomies 拆開驗證）
- PSTZ 2014 的 industry-level finding 在 Berk-Green 模型裡並不天然，是 PSTZ 額外提的擴充
- 兩者一起看才完整：理論說「為什麼 alpha 會 = 0」、實證說「具體是哪個 channel 在起作用」
